Who we are

prima.it

insurance / technology

Our mission is to use technology to disrupt an old-fashioned business like insurance. We like to standout from the crowd of lazy mercenaries that dominate this industry.

We are the only insurance company in the world with software 100% developed in house. From the front-end to the backoffice, going through the pricing engine, everything has been developed by our technology team in a 18 months effort and keeps evolving.

Our whole infrastructure is in cloud on AWS. We develop mainly in PHP with Symfony and in Javascript with AngularJS. The outcome is an IT performance 5 times higher than the one of the peers at one hundreth of the costs.

Agile

We develop with agile methodologies. We hate functional silos and we encourage close collaboration between coders and business owners.

Cloud

We have pioneered the usage of AWS, where we have built our entire platform. Nontheless Prima.it has been chosen as a case study at international cloud conferences.

Code

We love to write beautiful code, optimized to achieve high performance no matter the external constraints. We get excited to discover and try new languages everyday.

Tools

It's incredibly important to use the right tools. Every developer is allowed to choose his own fancy keyboard, to have at least two big monitors and a powerful machine.

Workplace

Our workplace fosters knowledge sharing and enhances a tremendous throughput of high quality software.

Location

We are based in piazza Duomo, the heart of Milan. We love to have meetings on a terrace with a view and enjoy a break walking in the centre of one of Italy's most beautiful city.

Leading Actor

Play a key role in shaping Prima.it within flexible structures and flat hierarchies; there is a great potential for seeing the difference your contribution makes!

Growth

We have an unlimited budget for conferences, books, and training. On a regular basis we get time to try something new, to enjoy hacking and open source.

Awesome Team

At Prima.it we believe that code is craft!
We're hardworking, creative and passionate about what we do and we're always looking for talented developers to join us.
